Presenting a document online.

By presenting my report in html, it is easier to produce and takes less time to present it to a wider audience.


The document 'planets', gives the reader an extensive view of the solar System, to make the report easier for the reader, I have included a 'Table of Contents' at the beginning of the report, where the reader can clearly see how the report is made up, and gives the reader a link to specific areas of the report, which they may need to access as a priority.


Also there is a 'List of figures', which catergorise the pictures included within the report, giving the picture a name, and highlights the page number within the report where the reader can find the picture.


At the end of the report,there is an 'Index', again showing the reader where they can fnd different elements of the report.


By posting the report online, the reader can access from where they are, on a computer, tablet or smart phone as long as they have the URL and an internet connection.they can download and save to their personel files for future reference.
